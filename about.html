About Pratap Prasuti Grih

Pratap Prasuti Grih is an infertility & A.R.T research, Thoracic and Laproscopic surgery center based in Bikaner of Rajasthan, India. It is being managed and run by eminent medical practitioners having special qualifications in IVF, Infertility, Gynae Endoscopy, Obstetrics and laproscopy. The hospital is installed with all the modern equipments required for specialities we provide.

  • More than 25 years of existance.
  • First Private Hospital of Town.
  • First ART baby of Town.
  • First VATS operation in Town.
  • First to start TVS.
  • Only hospital in division to provide facility of VATS and thoracic surgery.
  • Dr. Vikram Singh is the only member of ESHRE (Belgium) of Bikaner.
  • Dr.Suman is the only Gynaecological Sonologist of Bikaner having a Diploma in Gynae & Obst. Sonography (Europe).
  • All specialist doctors trained in Europe.

We specialize in each and every aspect of infertility and provide comprehensive services in IUI, IVF, IVF - egg donation, embryo donation, surrogate motherhood, male infertility, natural infertility treatments, semen banking and embryo freezing.
