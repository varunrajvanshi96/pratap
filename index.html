Pratap Prasuti Grih

We are an infertility clinic based in Bikaner, Rajasthan-India. We are into existence for 27 years and are focused on providing moral, emotional, ethical and most advanced technical support to couples trying out to find a solution to the image of infertility. We specialize in each and every aspect of infertility and provide comprehensive services in IUI, IVF, IVF - egg donation, embryo donation, male infertility, natural infertility treatments, semen banking, embryo freezing, sexual and psychological problem and try to give moral and emotional support to our infertility patients. We provide everything under one roof for our general and medical tourism patients and make best possible efforts to help our patients get pregnant in shortest possible time with continuous efforts and hard work of our highly competitive staff, a STATE OF THE ART embryology culture lab, availability of embryologist, round the clock, we are able to achieve an astounding success rate of 35-40%. We aim to provide most advanced, competitive services of international standards at an affordable price and try to help you achieve your goal by providing practical alternative solutions
