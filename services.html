Initial Consultation & Evaluation

Since infertility can develop as a result of both male and/or female problems, our comprehensive evaluation includes a personalized and in-depth consultation between the couple and our physicians. At this time, the physician will review the woman’s medical history, previous treatments and examinations, hormonal assessments, and other fertility testing. A complete semen analysis may be performed as part of the man’s evaluation and a hysterosalpingogram may be recommended for the woman to investigate the possibility of uterine and fallopian tube abnormalities. Based on these initial findings, a plan for fertility investigation will be discussed and initiated with the couple..

Important ! New patients need to provide all pertinent medical records and HSG X-rays to our office prior to their initial consultation.

Comprehensive Fertility Testing

TComprehensive fertility testing is available in our Center which includes a fully licensed and certified andrology laboratory for semen and sperm studies. Testing is individualized for each patient and may include:

  • Semen analysis with strict morphology studies Hormonal evaluations.
  • Hormonal evaluations.
  • Ovarian reserve testing.
  • Hysterosalpingograms.
  • Hysterosonograms.
  • Pelvic ultrasound.
  • Endometrial biopsies.

Nearly 40% of infertility problems result from a male factor. Many of these factors can be treated and overcome. If required, our physicians work closely with urologists who specialize in male infertility if further investigation and treatment is required

Ovulation Induction & Superovulation

To help increase the opportunity for conception, the use of oral or injectable medications may be recommended to stimulate the ovaries to produce and release mature eggs. This is a common treatment for women with unexplained infertility. For women who do not ovulate on their own, medications are prescribed to induce ovulation so that conception can occur with either timed intercourse or artificial insemination (IUI). All ultrasound monitoring occurs at our Center and is completed by our Reproductive Endocrinologists who specialize and are extensively trained and experienced in ultrasound techniques and interpretations.

Andrology Laboratory Services

Our licensed and certified andrology laboratory is staffed by andrologists who are specially trained and credentialed to assess all parameters of sperm and semen. In addition to providing comprehensive sperm testing, our Center andrology laboratory provides superior methods for evaluating sperm for analysis and processing or washing sperm for intrauterine inseminations (IUIs). It is these specialized methods which contribute to higher fertility success rates from inseminations.

Services performed by our Andrology Laboratory include:

  • Semen Analysis
  • Semen Culture
  • Sperm Washing
  • Cryopreservation

Our laboratory is equipped to maintain the short term storage of Donor semen specimens for donor insemination services.

The Centers collection room was specifically designed to offer our male patients privacy, comfort, and convenience for producing semen specimens. Appointments can be made from 7:30 am onward to help accommodate working schedules.

Assisted Reproductive Technology (ART)

Beginning with the birth of the first IVF baby, ART has opened the doors for many women who could not conceive. The development of ART in recent years has become a foundation for highly successful fertility treatments and has resulted in tens of thousands of babies being born

Our IVF Program gives our patients access to world class ART services including:

  • IVF In Vitro Fertilization.
  • Blastocyst Culturing.
  • Embryo Cryopreservation.
  • Donor Oocyte (Egg) Services Oocyte Cryopreservation (Egg Freezing) Services
  • Oocyte Cryopreservation (Egg Freezing) Services

All IVF monitoring, inclusive of all blood and ultrasound monitoring as well as physician management, of the IVF cycle takes place at the Pratap Prasuti Grih for our patient"s convenience as well as continuity of care. Working closely as a team with our physicians, PPG's medical and embryology staff are experts in these specialized ART laboratory techniques.The embryologists are responsible for the preparation and fertilization of sperm and eggs in the laboratory and for the development of these embryos until it is time to transfer them back into the uterus.

We believe the combination of the convenience, comfort, and personalized services our IVF patients receive from the Pratap Prasuti Grih along with the expertise and success rates of the PPG physicians ART services, are a winning combination for our patients.

Surgical Services

Surgical procedures may be performed as a treatment for infertility and may reduce or eliminate the need for assisted reproductive technologies (ART). Our physicians have years of experience in reproductive surgical techniques used to treat fallopian tube obstructions, pelvic adhesions, endometriosis, fibroids, and uterine anomalies. Operative laparoscopy and hysteroscopy can often correct some of these related problems of infertility.

Patient Education & Support Services

Our nursing and medical staff works closely with each patient to help the patient or couple best understand their fertility options and treatments. Patients are supplied with written educational materials necessary to answer all of their questions and to ensure that partners maintain a complete understanding and comfort level with the treatments. Comprehensive IVF teaching sessions are held for patients undergoing in vitro fertilization and all patients have access to our fertility lending library.

Achieving a pregnancy may have significant emotional impact on a couple. Since emotional health is just as important as physical health, supportive counseling services are available to the couple as well. All counseling sessions are conducted in a discreet and confidential manner.
